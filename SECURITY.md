# Security Policy
Please carefully review the information below incase you find a vulnerability in our platform.
## Reporting a vulnerability
To report a vulnerability in the QADS.cloud website, please visit https://qads.cloud/contact.html or contact us directly at help@qads.cloud. Thank you!
## Privately reporting a vulnerability
Don't worry, all reports are private. If you want extra security, contact us directly to our email. No contributors can access it.
