The Evolution from Wi Images to QADS.cloud

November 27, 2025

1. The reason behind the pivot

Our transition from Wi Images to QADS.cloud marks a significant shift in our strategic focus. While Wi Images began as a passion project for free image hosting, it rapidly scaled to host over 1.5 million imagesâ€”far exceeding the capacity of our original infrastructure. To ensure reliability and innovation, we are moving away from simple file hosting to focus on next-generation web technology.

2. What is QADS.cloud?

QADS.cloud is a state-of-the-art, open-source website builder designed to democratize web design. We believe that powerful web tools should be accessible, transparent, and community-driven. Our platform leverages the latest frontend technologies to offer an intuitive visual editor backed by clean, exportable code. Whether you are a developer looking for flexibility or a business owner needing performance, QADS.cloud provides the open foundation you need to build the modern web.

3. Important notice for legacy users

For those who have hosted content on the Wi platform, we have established a generous sunset period. Your existing images will remain accessible until December 1st, 2028. After this date, all legacy data will be permanently purged. For future hosting needs, we recommend migrating your assets to dedicated services such as ImgBB (note: this is a suggestion, not a sponsorship).
