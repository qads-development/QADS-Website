Prohibited Businesses on QADS.cloud

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

To maintain a safe, legal, and compliant service environment, QADS.cloud strictly prohibits the creation, hosting, or promotion of websites and businesses that fall into the following categories. This list is not exhaustive, and we reserve the right to determine if a business activity is unacceptable. Hosting any of the following content will result in immediate account termination, with no refund.

Prohibited Content and Services

The following businesses and content are explicitly forbidden on the QADS.cloud platform:

  • Illegal Activities: Websites promoting, facilitating, or engaging in any activity that is illegal under Canadian law, the laws of Quebec, or the jurisdiction in which the user is based.
  • High-Risk Financial Services:
    • Gambling, betting, or any games of chance (e.g., casinos, sports betting, lotteries).
    • Investment services, financial advice, or financial planning without proper legal licensing and regulatory compliance.
    • Cryptocurrency exchanges, mining operations, ICOs, or any form of crypto investment platform (e.g., NFTs, tokens).
    • Multi-Level Marketing (MLM) or pyramid schemes.
  • Controlled and Regulated Goods:
    • The sale, distribution, or promotion of firearms, ammunition, explosives, or destructive devices.
    • The sale or promotion of any illegal drugs, controlled substances, or drug paraphernalia.
    • The sale or promotion of all nicotine products, including but not limited to vapes, e-cigarettes, tobacco, and nicotine patches.
  • Adult and Exploitative Content:
    • Pornography, sexually explicit content, or any sexually oriented material, including but not limited to adult merchandise, escort services, or non-consensual sexual content.
    • Content involving the sexual exploitation of children or any form of child abuse.
  • Hate Speech and Harassment:
    • Any content that promotes hatred, discrimination, violence, or disparagement against groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or veteran status.
    • Content designed to harass, bully, or intimidate individuals.
  • Malware and Hacking: Websites promoting or distributing malicious software, viruses, or tools designed to gain unauthorized access to computer systems, data, or networks.
  • Copyright/Trademark Infringement: Websites dedicated to the distribution or sale of content that violates intellectual property rights (e.g., pirated software, counterfeit goods).

Compliance and Enforcement

By using QADS.cloud, you agree to:

  1. Self-Monitor: Ensure that your website and business activities are in full compliance with these Prohibited Business terms at all times.
  2. Cooperate: Provide QADS.cloud with any necessary information requested during an investigation into potential violations.

We actively monitor our platform. If your website is found to host or promote any prohibited content or business, we will take immediate action, which includes the removal of the content and the termination of your account as outlined in our Terms of Service.

If you believe a site hosted on QADS.cloud is in violation of these terms, please contact us immediately at help@qads.cloud.
