We are switching from Wi Images to QADS.cloud

November 22, 2025 - 2 minutes read

1. Why we switched

We switched from Wi Images to QADS.cloud for multiple reasons, but mainly because we aren't an image service anymore. Wi Images was a free image hosting platform, but it was only meant to be a side project. It grew to over 1,5M hosted images, which is not at all what we expected. The database and server ran off my own computer. We are switching to QADS.cloud to build a full and operational service.

2. What is QADS.cloud?

QADS.cloud puts our team of developers to work by developing websites for small businesses. Some growing companies cannot afford a big development team, so we build a website for them at a low cost. We also offer a Suite.

3. What should I do?

If you hosted images on Wi, you have until December 1st, 2028 before we remove them. We recommended ImgBB, which is a free online hosting service. (Not endorsed or sponsored)
